Access Creates The Opportunity to Create

A student-founded nonprofit ensuring every child has free access to STEM, robotics, and wellness education.

Who We Are

ACTOC is a youth-led nonprofit founded by high school students committed to making education truly accessible. We believe a child’s future should never be limited by income, location, language, or awareness.

The Problem

STEM Access Gap

Many students never access STEM due to cost, lack of programs, distance, or awareness. Opportunities exist—but not equally.

Wellness Gap

Nutrition, mental health, and physical wellness education are often missing, leaving students without essential life knowledge.

Our Solution

STEM & Robotics Workshops

Free hands-on workshops brought directly to schools. No cost. No experience needed. Just opportunity.

Wellness Series

Books and events teaching nutrition, health, and mental wellness in an engaging, accessible way.

Impact

Cost to schools: $0
Cost to families: $0

Building confidence, awareness, and opportunity for every child we reach.
